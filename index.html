Digital Payments: The way forward

This year, we are focusing on digital payments and the specific problem of products in this space -- breaking the urban barrier. Rolling out a payment system is no easy feat. The diversity of the Indian market makes it even more complex. Most products face the challenge of scaling - not just in volume, but also in terms of reach to people in the depths of rural India.


This Converge, participants will hear from industry experts about the challenges they faced in achieving scale and success. They will also talk about the other aspects like trends, security and policy within this domain.

The event is open for all, and we expect the audience to be a diverse group of engineers, business analysts, product managers and entrepreneurs.

Hope to see you on Saturday, 21st April @ ThoughtWorks, Whitefield Bangalore
