Localising Digital Payments

This year, we wanted to focus on “Digital Payments” and the specific problem of products in this space which is to break the urban barrier. Most products face the challenge of scaling - not just in terms of volume but also in terms of reach to rural areas. In a time where technology shapes government policy, our decisions as engineers has never been more critical.


Come be part of Converge, that aims to bring together people like you, who are passionate about learning current trends in the market, sharing your views and ideas, and networking with industry experts.
